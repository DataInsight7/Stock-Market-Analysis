# Stock-Market-Analysis

Overview

A data analytics project focused on evaluating stock market performance, trends, and investment opportunities using data-driven techniques and financial indicators.

Objective

Evaluate stock and market performance

Identify profitable investment opportunities

Analyze market trends and volatility

Assess risk for informed decision-making

Forecast future price movements using indicators

Tools

Excel | SQL | Power BI | Tableau

KPIs

Total Market Capitalization – Overall market size and economic strength

Trading Volume – Number of shares traded in a given period

Portfolio % Allocation – Asset distribution to manage risk

Dividend Yield – Annual dividend income vs share price

Trade Win Ratio – Trading strategy effectiveness

Sharpe Ratio – Risk-adjusted return measurement

Key Insights

High market volatility observed during multiple periods

Trading volumes spike during volatile market phases

Investors shifted from growth stocks to dividend-paying stocks

Liquidity improved price discovery but increased intraday swings

Low-volume stocks show price inefficiencies

Momentum-driven investor behavior dominates short-term moves

Strong stocks offer long-term opportunities during corrections

Recommendations

Focus on fundamentally strong stocks during market dips

Maintain portfolio diversification to reduce risk

Monitor macroeconomic and policy changes closely

Use technical indicators for better entry and exit points

Balance growth and dividend stocks for stability

Deliverables

Excel Dashboard

Power BI Dashboard

Tableau Dashboard

SQL Queries

Stock Performance Report

Impact

Supports smarter buy/sell/hold decisions

Reduces investment risk

Improves long-term portfolio returns

Enhances understanding of market behavior
